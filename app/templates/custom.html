Using Your Own Text

The Auto-Worksheet app can accept copy-and-pasted text.
The app will treat every double-new line as a paragraph for the purposes of generating fill-in-the-blank questions. Make sure your text is formatted well to avoid any issues!

You can use the following sources of text in the Auto-Worksheet app:

  • A custom-written document
  • Any document on your computer
  • Anything else you can copy and paste!
  • Turn a scanned page into text with OCR and paste it in
  • Download the subtitles for YouTube videos and generate a listening/discussion worksheet!
